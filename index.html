Comments

If implemented well

Merits

  • help the people who are not placental physiologist or pathologist to “easily” recognize cell and tissue types from HE slides (and report in the publication)
  • add the estimated cell type proportion

But

  • ground truth is from the experts
  • a compromise with the price of time, communication, human resources in experts’ check

Medical readout - If the pathological changes are obvious, no need - If they are subtle, any significance?
